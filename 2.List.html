Reviews of My Favorite Books

  1. Fourth Wing

    Fourth Wing written by Rebecca Yarros is a Romantascy - A Fantasy romance follows Xaden Riorson, a rebellion leader, and Violet Sorrengail, the daughter of a general, as they navigate a battle academy where riders bond with dragons. It's a mix of mystery, romance, fantasy, and emotional connections, highlighting friendships and the evolving romance between the leads. With dragons and signet powers at the core, the novel ends on a cliffhanger. It is the first book in a five-book series.

  2. A Good Girl's Guide to Murder

    A Good Girl’s Guide to Murder written by Holly Jackson is a gripping murder mystery that follows Pippa Fitz-Amobi, aka Pip, a college student who investigates the murder of Andy Bell for her podcast. Partnering with Ravi Singh, the brother of the accused, Pip uncovers dark secrets and hidden truths that twist the case in unexpected ways. The story masterfully blends suspense, friendship, and hints of romance, with a strong focus on Pip’s determination and growth. The book also has two sequels that continues to follow Pip and Ravi.

  3. Red Queen

    Red Queen written by Victoria Aveyard is a gripping dystopian fantasy set in a world where Silver-blooded people have powers, and Red-blooded people do not. Mare Barrow, a Red, unexpectedly discovers she has a powerful ability, thrusting her into the dangerous royal court. With twists, romance, and political intrigue, the story keeps readers hooked. Ending on a cliffhanger, it sets the stage for an exciting sequel.
